About Andrew

I am a Canadian born machine learning researcher and Aerospace engineer. I have lived and studied in Canada, Japan, Sri Lanka and Switzerland. I am a native English speaker but can get by with French or Japanese in informal settings. I am married and we have two wonderful boys.

My research interests include machine learning, robotics, signal processing and orbital dynamics. I have experience with spacecraft qualification testing, in-flight measurements, environmental noise controllers, dataset image rendering, machine learning and network compression. Links to the various relevant projects can be found on this site under "Projects".
